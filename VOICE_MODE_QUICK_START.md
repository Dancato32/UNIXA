# Voice Mode Quick Start Guide

## How to Use Voice Mode

### 1. Activate Voice Mode
- Click the "Voice Mode" button in the top-right corner of the chat interface
- The dual-panel interface will open:
  - **Left Panel**: Voice controls and AI avatar
  - **Right Panel**: Solution board

### 2. Grant Microphone Permission
- Your browser will ask for microphone access
- Click "Allow" to enable voice input
- Note: Requires HTTPS or localhost

### 3. Ask a Question
- Wait for the "Listening..." status
- Speak your question clearly (e.g., "Solve 2x + 3 = 7")
- The system will capture your speech automatically

### 4. Watch and Listen
- The AI will:
  - Display your question on the board
  - Show "Thinking..." while processing
  - Speak the solution step-by-step with natural voice
  - Display each step on the board as it's explained
  - Highlight the current step being spoken
  - Show the final answer in a highlighted box

### 5. Continue the Conversation
- After the AI finishes speaking, it automatically starts listening again
- Ask follow-up questions or new problems
- The board will clear for each new problem

## Controls

### Voice Panel Buttons:
- **⏹ Stop Speaking**: Stops the AI voice immediately
- **🎤 Start Listening**: Manually start listening (usually auto-starts)
- **✕ Close**: Exit Voice Mode and return to chat

### Board Panel Buttons:
- **Clear Board**: Manually clear the board content

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Speak Clearly**: Enunciate your words for better recognition
2. **Use Complete Sentences**: "Solve 2x + 3 = 7" works better than "2x + 3 = 7"
3. **Wait for Status**: Make sure it says "Listening..." before speaking
4. **Quiet Environment**: Reduce background noise for better recognition
5. **Math Notation**: Say "x squared" instead of "x^2", "one half" instead of "1/2"

## Troubleshooting

### Microphone Not Working:
- Check browser permissions (click lock icon in address bar)
- Ensure you're on HTTPS or localhost
- Try refreshing the page
- Check if another app is using the microphone

### Voice Not Speaking:
- Check your device volume
- Ensure browser has permission to play audio
- Try clicking "Stop Speaking" then ask again

### Steps Not Appearing:
- Ensure the question requires step-by-step solution
- Try asking a math or science problem
- Check that JavaScript is enabled

### Recognition Errors:
- Speak more slowly and clearly
- Reduce background noise
- Try rephrasing your question
- Use simpler language

## Privacy Note

Voice input is processed by your browser's built-in speech recognition API. Audio is not stored or transmitted to external servers beyond the text transcription.
