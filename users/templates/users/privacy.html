Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Paw, we genuinely care about your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, process, and safeguard your information when you use our educational AI platform, tools, and social features.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you when you register, use our AI tools, or participate in the community:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, educational institution, and profile photo.
  • Study Materials: Documents, slides, PDFs, and notes you upload to the platform for AI analysis, quizzes, or audio studio features.
  • Community Activity: Posts, comments, likes, study group participation, and messages sent within the Paw Network.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our AI Tutor, session times, feature utilization, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data powers your personalized learning experience:

  • To operate and maintain the AI Tutor, providing personalized answers based on your specific uploaded materials.
  • To generate dynamic study assets such as quizzes, podcasts, and summaries.
  • To connect you with classmates and suggest study groups based on your courses and institution.
  • To improve our AI models securely (your personal documents are strictly siloed and never used to train global generative models).

3. Data Protection and AI Usage

We apply strict partitioning to student data. The documents and notes you upload remain yours. They are processed ephemerally by our AI engine to generate your study materials (like smart briefings and podcast audio) but are never exposed to other users unless explicitly shared by you in a public community feed or shared workspace.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, export, or permanently delete your account and all associated study materials. You can exercise these rights directly from your account settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our student data practices, please contact us at privacy@nexa-paw.com.
