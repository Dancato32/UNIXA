Platform Security

Your academic life, protected with enterprise-grade security.

Thousands of students trust Paw with their notes, assignments, and study materials. We employ rigorous protocols to ensure your data stays safe, private, and out of the wrong hands.

End-to-End Encryption in Transit

All data sent between your browser and Paw's servers (including chat messages, uploaded documents, and file downloads) is securely encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS 1.2+. Every interaction operates strictly over HTTPS.

Secure Cloud Storage

Your PDFs, generated podcast audio files, and profile media are stored sequentially in heavily audited, encrypted Cloudinary buckets. We utilize advanced tokenized delivery mechanisms so only you (or members of your explicit shared workspaces) can view or stream your documents.

Authentication and Identity

Paw uses session-based authentication reinforced with secure HTTP-only cookies to protect you against session hijacking and Cross-Site Scripting (XSS). We enforce CSRF validation on all state-changing API operations (such as making a post or updating an AI prompt).

Responsible AI and Ephemeral Processing

When you chat with your Neural Tutor, we only inject the specific document snippets necessary to answer your question. We never submit your entire vault of documents to language models simultaneously. Furthermore, our LLM partners are strictly prohibited from saving, observing, or training their base models on your personal Paw uploads.

Vulnerability Response

Security is a perpetual process. If you are a white-hat security researcher and believe you have discovered a vulnerability within the Paw ecosystem, please do not disclose it publicly. Email us directly at security@nexa-paw.com for a safe harbor process and potential bounty reward.
